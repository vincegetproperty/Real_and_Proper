Frequently Asked Questions

What is real estate tokenization?

It’s the process of converting real estate assets into digital tokens, allowing fractional ownership and trading on blockchain networks.

Is this secure?

Yes, all transactions are recorded on blockchain, ensuring transparency and security for investors.

Can I sell my tokens?

Yes. Our platform enables peer-to-peer trading of property tokens, improving liquidity compared to traditional real estate.

What’s the minimum investment?

Invest as little as R5000 in fractional shares, making property ownership accessible to everyone.
